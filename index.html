Rectangles

Try this out!

Welcome to Rectangles - a simple tool for drawing rectangles. To draw a rectangle, click on the canvas and drag, extending it. When you're satisfied with the shape, simply let go. If two rectangles intersect - we'll let you know!

To delete a rectangle, click on the Delete button to enter the delete mode, then click on the figure you want to remove. Once you're satisfied with the current state of your canvas and want to go back to adding shapes - press Add.

If you want to start from scratch, simply click on Clear to reset the canvas.

Bored of the classy black? Click on the Change colour button to change it to something lighter!

Enjoy!
